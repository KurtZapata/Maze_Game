GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

1. Objective: - Your goal is to navigate through the maze labyrinth and reach the exit point.
2. Controls:
- Use the arrow keys or on-screen directional buttons to move your character:
- ↑ (Up)
- ↓ (Down)
- ← (Left)
- → (Right)
3. Avoid Obstacles: - Be careful of obstacles such as walls or barriers. You cannot pass through them.
- Colliding with obstacles may result in penalties such as losing health or getting slowed down.
4. Collect Items: - Explore the maze to collect useful items like keys, potions, or tools.
- Some items may help you unlock doors, restore health, or overcome challenges.
5. Health and Status: - Monitor your character's health, stamina, and other attributes displayed on the screen.
- Avoid hazards and replenish health with collected items if needed.
6. Find the Exit: - Locate the exit marked by a specific goal or destination within the maze.
- Reach the exit to advance to the next level or complete the game.
